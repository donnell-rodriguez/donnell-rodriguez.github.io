EnvPool: A Highly Parallel Reinforcement Learning Environment Execution Engine

EnvPool is a high-performance parallel environment executor for reinforcement learning workloads. It is designed to accelerate RL agent training by executing environments efficiently through C++ thread pools, pybind11 integration, and vectorized environment interfaces.

My related work focuses on understanding, maintaining, and applying high-throughput RL environment execution patterns for reinforcement learning training and deployment workflows.
